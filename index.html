Secure networking

MCNF — Magic Mesh

A secure, no-fixed-center workgroup mesh — with native Rust, IBM-Carbon-styled GUIs — for stock Fedora Cosmic. Up to eight machines form one private encrypted workgroup with no central server: any node can author fleet policy, every node enforces it, running over a Nebula overlay with etcd coordination and Syncthing replication.
