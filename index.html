MAP2 — Mackes Audio Platform 2

A modular, real-time audio processing platform that turns commodity Linux hardware into a professional-grade guitar and audio rig. A C++ JUCE engine, Python FastAPI backend, and React dashboard combine to deliver neural amp modeling, convolution reverb, and a full effects chain at single-digit millisecond latency — with multi-node clustering over Ethernet for scaling a whole band on one shared digital backbone.

Mesh-native desktop OS

MDE Construct

A secure, no-fixed-center workgroup mesh — and the egui-native, DRM-native desktop that runs on it. Up to eight machines form one private, encrypted workgroup with no central server: any node can author fleet policy, every node enforces it. On top sits a thin client that brokers full VM desktops — Windows, Ubuntu and more — rendered natively over the mesh, all on a Nebula overlay with etcd coordination and Syncthing replication.
